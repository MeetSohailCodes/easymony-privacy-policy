1. Who We Are

AutoDeal is a mobile app designed to help dealers and similar users manage vehicle inventory, store dealer profile details, capture and attach vehicle images, manage related documents, and export or share inventory-related information.

The data controller or responsible publisher for the app is: [REPLACE WITH YOUR LEGAL NAME OR BUSINESS NAME].

2. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to information accessed through the AutoDeal mobile app, including data entered by users, images and files selected or captured by users, locally stored app data, and user-initiated export or sharing actions.

As of this version of AutoDeal, the app is primarily a local-device inventory management app. Based on the current implementation, dealer profile data, vehicle records, attached photos, and related documents are stored locally on the user’s device unless the user chooses to export, share, or back up that information.

3. Data We Access

3.1 Information you enter

AutoDeal may access and store information that you provide, such as:

  • Dealer profile details, including name, workshop name, email, phone number, and profile image.
  • Vehicle inventory records, including make, model, year, registration, chassis number, engine number, pricing, mileage, city, and status.
  • Vehicle-related notes, custom fields, smart card details, and CNIC-related images or document records if you add them.
  • Favorites, preferences, and inventory display settings.

3.2 Photos, documents, and media

If you use AutoDeal’s camera, gallery, image picker, or document picker features, the app may access:

  • Photos you capture with your device camera for dealer or vehicle records.
  • Images you select from your device gallery.
  • Documents and files you select to attach to vehicle records or use in import and export workflows.

3.3 Exported and shared content

When you use the app’s export, backup, or sharing features, AutoDeal may generate files that include app data, such as CSV files, PDF files, ZIP backup files, inventory cards, images, and related metadata.

3.4 Technical and device-level data

The app may access limited technical information needed to operate, such as local file paths, permission status, and app configuration values. Based on the current app implementation, AutoDeal does not include a dedicated analytics SDK, advertising SDK, or a remote user account platform.

4. How We Use Data

AutoDeal uses data to provide the app’s core functionality, including:

  • Creating, editing, displaying, and organizing vehicle inventory records.
  • Capturing and attaching photos for vehicle entries and dealer profile images.
  • Attaching images and documents to vehicle-related records.
  • Generating exports, backups, PDFs, CSVs, and shareable inventory content at your request.
  • Saving settings and preferences locally on the device.
  • Importing or restoring backups that you choose to load into the app.

AutoDeal is not intended to sell personal data, and based on the current version of the app, user-entered inventory data is not automatically transmitted to the developer’s own backend servers.

5. Device Permissions

5.1 Camera

AutoDeal requests camera access so users can capture photos for dealer profile images, vehicle images, and other inventory-related photo records directly inside the app.

5.2 Photo library or media access

AutoDeal may request access to photos or media files so users can choose existing images from the device and attach them to vehicle or profile records.

5.3 Files and documents

AutoDeal may request access to files or documents so users can import backup files, attach documents, export app data, or save backup files to a location the user selects.

5.4 Microphone or audio-related permissions

Some Android builds may include camera-related library permissions such as audio recording support. AutoDeal’s current intended inventory flow is focused on image and document handling. If a future version uses audio or video capture, this policy should be updated accordingly.

5.5 Internet access

The app may use network-related permissions for limited functionality such as opening external links, resolving app dependencies, or enabling supported framework features. However, based on the current version of the app, core inventory records are primarily handled locally on the device.

6. Sharing and Disclosure

AutoDeal does not automatically publish or publicly disclose your inventory data. Information may be shared only in the following cases:

  • When you actively use a share feature to send an inventory card, file, PDF, CSV, image, or backup through another app or service on your device.
  • When you choose to export data to local storage, cloud storage, email, messaging apps, or file-sharing apps available on your device.
  • When disclosure is required by applicable law, regulation, court order, or lawful government request.
  • When necessary to protect rights, safety, security, or prevent fraud or abuse.

Once you choose to share or export data outside AutoDeal, the privacy practices of the destination service, cloud provider, or third-party app will apply to that shared content.

7. Storage and Retention

Based on the current app implementation, AutoDeal stores app data locally on the user’s device using local device storage and local app databases or files. This may include dealer profile information, vehicle entries, favorites, documents, images, and backup artifacts.

Data is generally retained until you delete it, overwrite it, remove the app, clear app storage, or replace it through import or restore actions. Exported files and shared files may remain in the destinations you choose until you delete them separately.

8. Security

We aim to use reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect information processed by AutoDeal. However, no software, device, storage system, or transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

You are responsible for protecting your device, controlling who has access to it, and managing any files or backups you export or share.

9. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your device and applicable law, you may be able to:

  • Deny or revoke app permissions through your device settings.
  • Edit or delete dealer profile information and inventory records within the app.
  • Delete exported files or backups that you created.
  • Uninstall the app to remove local app data from the device, subject to any files you separately exported.
  • Contact the app publisher to make privacy-related inquiries.

10. Children’s Privacy

AutoDeal is not intended for children. If you believe that personal information from a child has been entered into the app or submitted to the publisher in error, contact us using the details below so the issue can be reviewed.
